About

I asked around for ideas for websites to create, and the best suggestion I got was a site with Creative Commons-licensed webcomics (Thanks, Blaine!) However, it turns out that there are very few webcomics that are under a Creative Commons license and thus that I could use on a website (list). Since I had only heard of two from this list, and since only one has a format that lends itself to stand-alone images, I'm left with only xckd.

Welcome to my website, and enjoy! The Facts page will give you some more information about webcomics, xkcd, and Creative Commons, and the Comics page will give you comics.
